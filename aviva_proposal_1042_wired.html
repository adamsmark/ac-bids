    About Adamsmark Construction

    For over 30 years in the construction industry, the founder of Adamsmark Construction and our team of committed professionals have managed an array of construction services. We understand all projects are unique. Our attention to communication and cooperation has fostered project success and long-term relationships with Owners, Management Companies, Architects, Engineers, and Subcontractors.

    Our unique position in fire restoration has proven our expertise in every aspect of the construction process — roofing, plumbing, electrical, and rehabbing both interior and exterior on existing buildings or building from the ground up.

    Adamsmark Construction is based in Fort Worth, Texas and is licensed in Texas, Louisiana, Florida, Georgia, and North & South Carolina. Our team has worked in nearly every state in the union.

      Warranties

      Adamsmark Construction LLC provides a One-Year warranty on workmanship, in addition to any warranties offered by the manufacturers of the products used, installed, or applied. All materials and equipment shall be of good quality and new, except as otherwise provided in the Contract Documents, and applied, installed, connected, erected, used, cleaned, and conditioned in accordance with the applicable supplier's instructions.

      The warranty is non-transferrable and does not cover surface abuse, neglect, damage from moisture penetration, natural catastrophic events, earth movement, or acts of God.

      Standard Exclusions (as Applicable)

      The following exclusions apply if not addressed in the Specific Scope of Work. Issues outside the Specific Scope will be managed by change order.

      • Hidden damage — damage not visible from ground level or until an outside surface is removed.
      • Mold and/or asbestos — any discovery not already indicated in the scope.
      • Previous damage — damage occurring prior to Adamsmark's work (e.g., overspray of paint, stucco, or EIFS).
      • Landscaping — all landscaping must be cut back at least 6 inches from the structure being worked on.
      • Architectural plans & renderings — costs for plans, blueprints, and renderings.
      • Property signs — removing, painting, repairing, or resetting signs and unit/building/carport numbers.
      • Certificates of Occupancy — the property remains in operation during renovation; a new CO is not required unless indicated.

      Payment & Acceptance

      Payment terms upon acceptance will be billed per a draw schedule to be determined. This proposal is valid if the project is contracted within 30 days of the original proposal date.
